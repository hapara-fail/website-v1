Support hapara.fail

Help us keep the lights on and continue developing resources!

hapara.fail is a passion project dedicated to providing free, open-source tools and guides that empower users to understand and navigate their digital environments, especially within educational settings like ChromeOS. We believe in digital literacy, user agency, and the importance of privacy.

Maintaining and expanding this site—developing new tools, writing in-depth guides, and covering hosting costs—requires time and resources. If you find our work valuable, please consider making a contribution. Every bit helps us continue our mission!
