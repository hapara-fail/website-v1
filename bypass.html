Support hapara.fail

Your contributions help us develop and share resources for a more open digital experience.

hapara.fail is a community-driven project dedicated to providing accessible guides, tools, and insights for users navigating controlled digital environments, with a focus on ChromeOS. Our goal is to foster understanding, promote digital literacy, and support user agency.

To continue creating valuable content, maintaining the site, and covering operational costs, we rely on the generosity of our community. If you find what we do helpful, please consider making a donation. Every contribution, large or small, makes a real difference and is deeply appreciated.

Open Source & Contributing

Beyond financial support, hapara.fail thrives on community involvement! Both this website and our associated tools, including the DNS blocklist, are open-source projects. We believe in transparency and the collective power of the community to build and refine resources that empower users.

If you're a developer, researcher, security enthusiast, or simply passionate about these topics, we warmly welcome your contributions. You can help by:

  • Suggesting new features or improvements for the site or tools.
  • Reporting bugs or issues you encounter through our issue trackers.
  • Contributing to our DNS blocklist with new domains that should be filtered or by reporting false positives.
  • Submitting code enhancements, documentation updates, or new tools via pull requests on GitHub.
